The Framework

01

One Hour a Day

Ideally 60 minutes. But if life gets crazy, 15 minutes of intentional focus is better than 0. It's about keeping the promise to yourself.

02

The "Cheat" Day

One day a week (recommended: Sunday) is for rest. You can use this to rot, touch grass, or catch up on sleep. Rest is part of the work.

03

Trust & Accountability

There is no teacher checking your homework. You can start a blog, use a Habit Tracker, post to GitHub, or just tell a friend. The only person you are cheating is Future You.
